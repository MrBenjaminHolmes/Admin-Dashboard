Desert Sunset

Learn how to paint a dramatic desert sunset with vibrant colors and striking silhouettes of cacti and mountains. This project focuses on color gradients and contrast.

Winter Wonderland

Capture the serene beauty of a winter landscape with snow-covered trees and a frozen lake. This project highlights the use of cool colors and texture for snow.

Quiet Cabin

Paint a cozy, quiet cabin nestled in the woods, complete with a smoking chimney and surrounding trees. This project focuses on building structure and detail.

Sunlit Meadow

Create a bright and cheerful sunlit meadow filled with wildflowers and grasses. This project emphasizes light and shadow to bring the scene to life.

Majestic Waterfall

Learn how to paint a majestic waterfall cascading down rocks into a serene pool. This project teaches techniques for creating movement and texture in water.

Forest Pathway

Paint a peaceful forest pathway surrounded by tall trees and dappled sunlight. This project focuses on perspective and depth to create a realistic scene.
